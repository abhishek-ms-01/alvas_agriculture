

# Alva's Agricultural College – Premium Website

## Overview
A visually stunning, modern college website with cinematic animations, rich imagery, and a premium university aesthetic. The site will use a green, earthy, white, and gold color palette with glassmorphism effects, parallax scrolling, and smooth micro-interactions throughout.

---

## Design System
- **Colors**: Deep green, earthy brown, crisp white, and gold accents with dark/light section contrast
- **Style**: Glassmorphism cards, soft shadows, rounded corners, gradient overlays
- **Typography**: Premium serif headings + clean sans-serif body text
- **Animations**: Scroll-triggered fade/slide animations, hover effects, parallax backgrounds, count-up numbers, auto-sliding carousels

---

## Pages & Sections (Single-Page with Smooth Scroll + Gallery Page)

### 1. Header
- Sticky transparent navbar that becomes solid on scroll
- College logo on left, animated menu links on right
- Mobile hamburger menu with slide-in drawer
- Menu: Home | About | Courses | Admissions | Facilities | Research | Gallery | Placements | Contact

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-screen slideshow background with agriculture/campus imagery (using high-quality Unsplash photos)
- Animated headline: *"Cultivating Knowledge. Harvesting the Future."*
- Sub-headline with college name
- Two animated CTA buttons: **Apply Now** and **Explore Campus**
- Floating animated leaf/plant icons
- Scroll-down bounce indicator

### 3. About College
- Split layout: image on one side, text on the other
- Animated count-up statistics (Years of Excellence, Students, Faculty, Research Projects)
- Chairman spotlight: Dr. M. Mohan Alva with glowing border image and animated quote

### 4. Courses Section
- Animated card grid with hover flip effect
- Courses: B.Sc Agriculture, M.Sc Agriculture, Diploma Programs
- Each card shows course image, duration, eligibility, and "Learn More" button

### 5. Facilities Section
- Horizontal scrolling gallery of facilities
- Smart Classrooms, Labs, Research Farms, Library, Hostel, Transportation
- Hover zoom with text overlay reveal

### 6. Research & Innovation
- Parallax background section
- Showcase cards for agricultural research, sustainable farming, organic practices
- Animated progress bars for research metrics
- Interactive timeline of milestones

### 7. Gallery Section
- Masonry grid layout with category filters (Campus, Labs, Fields, Events, Students)
- Lightbox popup on click
- Smooth zoom and fade animations
- Heavy use of high-quality agriculture and campus imagery

### 8. Admissions Section
- Eye-catching gradient CTA banner
- 3-step animated process (Apply → Entrance → Confirmation)
- Download brochure button
- Admission enquiry form with floating labels and validation

### 9. Placements & Career
- Auto-scrolling logo carousel of recruiters
- Animated success story cards
- Graphical placement statistics with charts

### 10. Student Life
- Full-width image sections
- Cards for clubs, events, NSS, sports
- Hover animations and testimonial quotes

### 11. Contact Section
- Embedded Google Map
- Animated contact form
- Address and contact details
- Social media icons with hover glow effect

### 12. Footer
- Dark themed with college logo, quick links, contact info
- Copyright notice
- Smooth scroll-to-top button

---

## Animations & Effects
- Scroll-triggered entrance animations on every section
- Parallax scrolling on hero and research sections
- Auto-sliding image carousels with smooth transitions
- Hover effects: card lifts, image zooms, underline animations, glow effects
- Count-up number animations on scroll
- Page load fade-in animation
- Floating decorative elements (leaves, water drops)
- Glassmorphism navbar and cards

## Responsive Design
- Fully responsive across mobile, tablet, and desktop
- Mobile-optimized navigation with drawer menu
- Touch-friendly carousels and galleries

## Image Strategy
- All images sourced from Unsplash (agriculture, campus, laboratory, nature themes)
- Full-width hero images with gradient overlays
- Optimized with lazy loading for performance
